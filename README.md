## About CapitalPrint

CapitalPrint is dedicated to redefining printing solutions. From **Sublimation** and **DTF Printing** to **UV Printing**, **School Uniforms**, **Funeral Cloths**, **Graphic Designs**, and **General Printing**, our services are tailored to meet individual and corporate branding needs. 

At CapitalPrint, we believe in **customizing the looks you wear** while ensuring the **highest quality designs** and **state-of-the-art printing technology**. Whether you're looking to promote your brand, outfit a team, or personalize your apparel, CapitalPrint is here to elevate your ideas.

## Features

### Home Page
- A **welcoming popup** introducing CapitalPrint’s services in a visually engaging grid layout.
- Stretchable **banner image** providing a clean and modern look.
- **Company description** with a dynamic paragraph and embedded drop cap logo.
- **Service links** for easy navigation to blog posts about each solution.

### Solutions Page
- Detailed **cards for each service** (e.g., Sublimated T-Shirts, Branded Uniforms, Corporate Uniforms, School Uniforms).
- Clear **description and modern design** of each offering.
- Direct links to **privacy policy** and additional resources in the footer.

### Modals and Popups
- **Order popup** for personalized requests, collecting required details like size and quantity.
- Enhanced **welcome popup** styled responsively for all screen sizes.

### WhatsApp Integration
- Seamless interaction with WhatsApp for quick communication and order placement.
